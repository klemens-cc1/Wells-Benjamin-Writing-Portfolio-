# Public Policy and International Affairs Portfolio
**Wells Benjamin** | University of Georgia

Welcome to my research and writing portfolio. This repository serves as a centralized collection of policy reports, academic research, and issue briefs focusing on international security, technological competition, and geopolitical analysis.

## Research Categories and Documents

### [F-35 Program and American Strategy](./F35-Policy-Report/)
A comprehensive policy report analyzing the industrial and organizational challenges of the F-35 program. This work evaluates 5th-generation airpower as a tool of strategic deterrence and addresses supply chain vulnerabilities in the defense industrial base.

### [The Lavender Veil: Digital Security](./Undergrad%20Thesis%20The%20Lavender%20Veil/)
An undergraduate research thesis exploring the emergence of covert digital security partnerships between Israel and Arab states. This paper utilizes a neoclassical realist framework to analyze the role of AI-driven intelligence and surveillance technology in regional power dynamics.

### [Syria as a Narco-State](./Syria-was-a-Narco-State/)
A report detailing the rise of the Captagon trade under the Assad regime. This analysis examines the transition of the Syrian state into a primary exporter of illicit stimulants as a mechanism to bypass international sanctions.

### [Wang Huning: China's Gray Eminence](./Wang%20Huning_%20China’s%20Gray%20Eminence/)
An issue brief profiling the chief ideological architect of the Chinese Communist Party. The brief analyzes Wang Huning’s influence on China's transition toward cultural nationalism and his role in shaping the "Chinese Dream."

### [Infectious Disease and Climate Change](./LJS%20Magazine%20Infectious%20Disease%20in%20a%20Changing%20World/)
An analytical essay on the intersection of environmental degradation and global health security. This work examines how climate change acts as a threat multiplier, exacerbating the spread of pathogens and widening global economic disparities.
