# Syria Was a Narco-State: The Rise of Captagon in the Middle East

## Document Details
* **Type**: Center for International Trade and Security (CITS) Report
* **Length**: 4 Pages 
* **Date**: May 2025

## Executive Summary
This report details the meteoric rise of Captagon, a synthetic stimulant that has come to dominate the Middle Eastern drug market and fund the Assad regime. It analyzes how economic isolation and the Syrian Civil War transformed the state into a primary producer and exporter of illegal psychotropic substances.

## Key Themes
* **Transnational Crime**: The evolution of Captagon from a legal medication to a global illicit network.
* **State-Sponsored Drug Trade**: How the Syrian regime utilized drug production to bypass international sanctions.
* **Regional Security**: The impact of drug trafficking on neighboring states and the potential for non-state actors to fill supply vacuums.
